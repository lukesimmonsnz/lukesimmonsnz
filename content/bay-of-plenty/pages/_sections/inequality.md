## Ethnic socioeconomic and health gaps in Bay of Plenty — Te Moana-a-Toi

The Bay of Plenty has significant intraregional income inequality. High-deprivation communities — concentrated in the eastern Bay (Ōpōtiki, Whakatāne) and parts of Rotorua — have median household incomes 25-30% below the regional average, high rates of material hardship, and limited access to the regional economy's higher-wage sectors.
