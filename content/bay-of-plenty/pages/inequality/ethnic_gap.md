## Claims cited on this page

- **Median household income in the Bay of Plenty's high-deprivation communities — concentrated in Ōpōtiki, Whakatāne, and parts of Rotorua — is approximately 25-30% below the regional average, reflecting occupational concentration in seasonal horticulture, limited tertiary access, and geographic isolation from higher-wage employment.** — Census 2023 Housing Characteristics.

Youth not in employment, education, or training (NEET) rates in high-deprivation communities are substantially above the national average.** *(confidence: medium)* — NIWA Regional Climate Projections 2024; Health and Wellbeing Survey 2023.
