## Earthquake-driven social stress

Post-quake, family violence increased 25% (2011-2013) as housing displacement, financial stress, and psychological trauma compounded pre-existing risk factors. While overall violence has stabilized, concentrations in high-deprivation areas remain acute.

### Integrated Community Safety Response

Integrated multi-agency response to crime (police, social services, justice, community) addressing root causes (deprivation, substance abuse, trauma) is more effective than enforcement alone.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish multi-agency safety hubs in high-crime areas (East Christchurch)
- Fund whānau-centered family violence prevention programs
