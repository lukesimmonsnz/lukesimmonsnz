- **Family violence (partner and child abuse) accounts for approximately 35-40% of reported violence-related offences in the Manawatū-Whanganui region. Whanganui city, with higher deprivation indices, records disproportionately higher family violence notification rates than Palmerston North or rural areas, correlating with socioeconomic stress factors.** [value: 37.5 percent; 2021-2023] *(confidence: medium)* — NZ Police Crime Statistics 2023: Manawatū-Whanganui Region; Whanganui City Council Annual Report 2023.
- **Manawatū-Whanganui has 3-4 domestic violence shelters operating at full or near-full capacity (Whanganui Women's Refuge, Palmerston North community services). Long waitlists and limited emergency accommodation gaps indicate unmet demand. Rural areas (Rangitikei, Tararua, Ruapehu) face significant access barriers to specialist support services.** *(confidence: medium)* — Health NZ: Manawatū-Whanganui Population Health Profile 2023; Whanganui City Council Annual Report 2023.
