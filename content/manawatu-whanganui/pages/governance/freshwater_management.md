- **Horizons Regional Council's One Plan (2024 update) is New Zealand's first integrated regional resource management strategy, combining land, water, and air policy. It mandates riparian protection, nitrogen-loss reduction targets (10% by 2030), and farm environment planning for all intensive farms. One Plan governs freshwater over the entire Manawatū-Whanganui region; compliance monitoring involves farmer consent orders and enforcement actions by regional rangers.** — One Plan: Horizons Regional Council Integrated Strategy; Horizons Regional Council Data and Research Services.
