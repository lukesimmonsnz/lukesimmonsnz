## Claims cited on this page

- **Local government reform proposals (2024) would consolidate Manawatū-Whanganui's seven territorial authorities into 2-3 larger councils to reduce administrative costs and improve service coordination. Proposed models include Palmerston North/Manawatū merger and separate Whanganui/Rangitikei/Horowhenua cluster. Councils estimate 10-15% cost savings but face implementation risks (staff redundancy, service delivery gaps during transition). Consultation with community is ongoing; reform implementation timeline uncertain (2025-2028 proposed).** *(confidence: medium)* — Palmerston North City Council Long-term Plan 2024-2034; Whanganui City Council Annual Report 2023.
