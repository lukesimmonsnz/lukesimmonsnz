## Claims cited on this page

- **Manawatū-Whanganui ranks 6th-7th regionally for overall socioeconomic deprivation (Stats NZ 2018 Index). Whanganui city is Quintile 5 (most deprived nationally); Palmerston North is mixed (Quintiles 2-3); rural areas vary (Rangitikei, Tararua, Ruapehu mostly Quintiles 3-4). Deprivation correlates with unemployment (rural unemployment 5-7%, urban 4-6%), household income (Whanganui median NZ$48,000 vs Palmerston North NZ$62,000), and access to services (health, education, transport). Cross-cutting deprivation reduction is a strategic priority across multiple councils.** — NZ Deprivation Index 2018: Manawatū-Whanganui Data; Census 2023: Manawatū-Whanganui Regional Profile.
