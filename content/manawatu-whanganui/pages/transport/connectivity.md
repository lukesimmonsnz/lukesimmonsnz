## Claims cited on this page

- **Regional connectivity in Manawatū-Whanganui relies primarily on road networks (SH1, SH2, SH3, SH56, SH57). SH1 passes through Palmerston North and Levin, connecting to Auckland (3 hours) and Wellington (2.5 hours). Rail connections (Wairarapa Line, Wellington-Hamilton route) link Palmerston North to other regions but passenger service is limited (daily Metlink service to Wellington, plus Scenic Great Northern Limited services). Bus services (Horizons, local contractors) provide regional connections but are declining in frequency. Road freight is dominant (98% of goods); rail carries minimal freight. Transport investment is highly vehicle-focused, limiting travel options for carless populations.** — NZTA: Manawatū-Whanganui Transport Strategy 2023; Horizons Regional Council Data and Research Services.
