# Cross-agency coordination deficit

<p class="horizon-band">Analysis horizon: 10yr · 50yr · 100yr</p>



## Scale and distribution

Poor coordination between central government, regional council, and district councils creates duplication, gaps, and slow responses.


## Key drivers

The primary drivers of cross-agency coordination deficit are structural and systemic, requiring both investment and institutional reform.

## Solution camps

A number of distinct positions recur in policy debates on this issue. Each is defensible on its own terms; none is obviously correct. Presented in alphabetical order without ranking.


### Local government reform and amalgamation

Amalgamating Northland's local authorities into a single unitary body would improve capacity and reduce duplication.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish a Northland unitary authority covering all district functions
- Transfer strategic planning and infrastructure functions to a single body
- Negotiate Māori wards and Treaty partnership seats into the new structure

**Tensions:**

- Loss of local representation and accountability to communities
- Transition costs and disruption to services during amalgamation
- Historical opposition to amalgamation from Northland communities

**Interventions on the system:**

- Establish a Northland unitary authority covering all district functions (state variable: `governance_pressure_index`, sign: +)


### Māori co-governance and Treaty-based partnership

Establishing genuine Treaty-based co-governance arrangements with Northland iwi improves legitimacy and local capacity.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish joint iwi-council committees with decision-making authority
- Fund iwi-led resource management and environmental governance
- Negotiate shared services arrangements between councils and iwi entities

**Tensions:**

- Co-governance requires deep institutional change and capability investment
- Political resistance from non-Māori ratepayers to shared authority
- Defining the scope and limits of co-governance is contested

**Interventions on the system:**

- Establish joint iwi-council committees with decision-making authority (state variable: `governance_pressure_index`, sign: +)
