### Iwi-Crown-Council Partnership Framework

Formalizing decision-making partnerships with Ngāi Tahu improves legitimacy and outcomes.

**Flagship moves:**

- Joint governance boards for key resources (water, forests, heritage)

- Colonial power imbalances; contentious process
