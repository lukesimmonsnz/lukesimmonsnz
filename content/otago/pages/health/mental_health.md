A number of distinct positions recur in policy debates on this issue. Each is defensible on its own terms; none is obviously correct. Presented in alphabetical order without ranking.


### Mental Health Service Expansion

Increasing mental health counselors, therapists, and peer support reduces waitlists and access barriers.

**Flagship moves:**

- Hire 100 additional mental health clinicians
- Expand 24/7 crisis response services
- Peer support worker program for Dunedin students

**Tensions:**

- Healthcare worker recruitment and retention
- Ongoing funding commitments

**Interventions on the system:**

- Reduce mental health service waitlist to <6 weeks (state variable: `mh_service_access`, sign: +)
