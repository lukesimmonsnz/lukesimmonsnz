### Rural GP Recruitment and Retention Incentives

Student loan forgiveness, housing subsidies, and clinical autonomy attract GPs to rural practices.

**Flagship moves:**

- Rural GP loan forgiveness scheme
- Housing subsidies for rural practices
- Extended professional development funding

**Tensions:**

- Ongoing subsidy costs and equity concerns
- Success dependent on rural lifestyle appeal
