## Solution camps

A number of distinct positions recur in policy debates on this issue. Each is defensible on its own terms; none is obviously correct. Presented in alphabetical order without ranking.


### Build-to-Rent and Rental Housing Investment

Encouraging institutional build-to-rent (BTR) and rental housing investment improves worker housing supply.

**Flagship moves:**

- Tax incentives for BTR operators
- Fast-track resource consents for BTR projects
- Develop BTR on council-owned land

**Tensions:**

- BTR investors prioritize market-rate rents; affordable element requires subsidy
- Tenant protection regulations reduce BTR investment attractiveness

**Interventions on the system:**

- Offer 10-year tax holiday for qualifying BTR projects (state variable: `rental_stock`, sign: +)
