## Health Outcomes and Health System Pressures

Southland has higher chronic disease burden; rural workforce shortages (GPs, nurses); mental health services oversubscribed; deprivation drives poor health outcomes.


### Overview

Southland has higher chronic disease burden; rural workforce shortages (GPs, nurses); mental health services oversubscribed; deprivation drives poor health outcomes.


### Structural drivers


**Rural Healthcare Access Barriers.** Distance, population sparsity, and recruitment difficulty limit health access.



### Solution camps

A number of distinct positions recur in the policy debate on this issue. Each is defensible on its own terms; none is obviously correct.


**Southland Health System Strengthening.** Strengthening health workforce and services improves health outcomes. Key moves include Rural health workforce recruitment and retention; Primary care access expansion; Health promotion and prevention programs. The main tensions are: Recruitment difficulty; Funding constraints.

## Rural Health Workforce Shortages

GPs, nurses, allied health scarce in rural Southland; Invercargill hospital competes with other regions; locum costs high; recruitment barriers high.


### Overview

GPs, nurses, allied health scarce in rural Southland; Invercargill hospital competes with other regions; locum costs high; recruitment barriers high.


### Structural drivers


**Rural Health Workforce Shortages.** Southland GPs aging; recruitment difficult; locum reliance high; specialist access limited.



### Solution camps
