## Claims cited on this page

- **Youth offending in Southland shows persistent structural drivers. Rural youth unemployment above regional average; limited post-school pathways in Gore and Winton contribute. Digital exclusion (limited broadband in remote areas) restricts opportunity access. Intervention services (counselling, skill-building) concentrated in Invercargill; rural youth access barriers.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Youth offending in Southland shows concentration in Gore and Winton. Youth justice referrals from secondary schools in smaller towns exceed Invercargill rates on per-capita basis. Limited youth employment/activity programmes outside Invercargill; rural youth service gaps documented. Substance misuse (alcohol, cannabis, methamphetamine) associated with offending.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Rural crime in Southland includes stock theft, agricultural equipment theft, and farm trespass. Farming communities from Invercargill to Mataura Valley report increasing property crime targeting irrigation infrastructure, pumps, and feed storage. Remote farm isolation (>20km from towns) increases offender opportunity.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Family violence in Southland shows elevated call-out rates. Southland Police district reports family violence call-outs above national average, particularly in Invercargill and rural isolation hotspots. Rural victim access to support services limited by distance; safe houses in Invercargill, Te Anau, Gore under resource pressure.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Crime trends in Southland show persistent methamphetamine-driven property crime in urban centres. Family violence remains elevated; police resourcing pressures limit prevention capacity. Youth offending in Gore and Winton correlated with rural unemployment and limited youth services. Alcohol-fuelled harm remains significant in Invercargill.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Crime safety in Southland shows regional variation. Invercargill CBD has elevated property crime and assault rates; methamphetamine supply driving property offences (burglary, shoplifting). Rural areas face stock theft and vehicle crime; Stewart Island faces isolation-related response delays. Southland Police district staffing at ~240 FTE for ~97,000 residents.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Family violence incidents in Southland are sustained above trend. Methamphetamine supply correlation with family violence documented; rural isolation exacerbates victim vulnerability. Police response time in remote areas (Catlins, Manapouri, Stewart Island) exceeds 2 hours in some cases; support service gaps in Te Anau and Riverton.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
- **Rural crime in Southland is sustained and dispersed. Coordinated regional farm watch schemes (Southland Rural Watch) report steady incident volume; methamphetamine-driven property crime increasing. Police rural liaison resources stretched; detection rates lower in remote districts than urban areas.** *(confidence: medium)* — Southland Regional Council Strategic Plan 2024.
