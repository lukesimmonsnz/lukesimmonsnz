### Market Stabilisation and Affordable Housing Supply

Building social and affordable housing in Invercargill addresses deprivation and prevents homelessness even in a low-price market.

**Flagship moves:**

- Fund Kāinga Ora build programme in Invercargill east
- Establish community land trust for affordable homeownership
- Renovate and retrofit existing state housing stock

**Tensions:**

- Low land prices reduce developer incentive — public subsidy needed to make affordable builds viable
- Population decline reduces the case for large-scale new supply

**Interventions on the system:**

- Commission 200 new social housing units in high-deprivation Invercargill suburbs (state variable: `social_housing_waitlist`, sign: -)
