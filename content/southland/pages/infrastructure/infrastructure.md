### Infrastructure Renewal and Climate Resilience

Prioritising maintenance and climate-proofing of Southland's ageing infrastructure prevents cascading failure in a low-density, remote region.

**Flagship moves:**

- Fund three-waters renewal in small Southland towns (Gore, Winton, Te Anau)
- Upgrade flood protection on key Southland rivers
- Extend fibre broadband to rural Southland

**Tensions:**

- Thin rate base makes infrastructure renewal unaffordable without central government co-investment
- Competing priorities between urban and rural infrastructure needs

**Interventions on the system:**

- Co-fund water infrastructure renewal for small Southland towns through three waters reform mechanisms (state variable: `infrastructure_condition_index`, sign: +)
