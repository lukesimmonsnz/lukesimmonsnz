### Income support and wage uplift

Increasing minimum wages and income-related rent subsidies provides direct relief to low-income households.

**Flagship moves:**

- Advocate for living wage as floor for all Waikato council and contracted workers
- Expand WFF and Accommodation Supplement uptake among eligible Waikato families
- Develop regional food resilience networks to reduce food insecurity

**Tensions:**

- Wage increases may reduce employment in low-margin sectors
- Income support alone does not address structural causes of poverty
