### Mining Transition and Green Economy Development

Coal mining decline is inevitable; West Coast needs proactive just-transition investment to develop alternative economic sectors including renewable energy, conservation economy, and value-add food production.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish West Coast Just Transition Fund from Crown-Māori partnership
- Develop pumped hydro or green hydrogen facility leveraging existing generation capacity
- Support conservation tourism economy through DOC partnership

**Tensions:**

- Mining communities have deep cultural attachment to the industry beyond economics
- Green economy development requires sustained long-term investment before employment returns
