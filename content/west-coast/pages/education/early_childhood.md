### Small School Sustainability and Digital Learning

West Coast's very small rural schools face closure pressure; digital learning hubs and multi-school clusters can sustain educational provision without large-scale consolidation.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish digital learning hub network across West Coast small schools
- Fund itinerant specialist teachers (arts, STEM, te reo Māori) across school clusters
- Provide high-quality fibre to all West Coast schools

**Tensions:**

- Digital learning cannot fully replace in-person specialist teaching
- Some families will still relocate for larger school options regardless of quality improvements

**Interventions on the system:**

- Fund digital learning cluster programme for West Coast small schools (state variable: `student_achievement_ncea`, sign: +)
