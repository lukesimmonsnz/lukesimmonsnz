### Tertiary Pathways and Vocational Training

West Coast lacks local tertiary provision; building vocational training pipelines for trades, mining transition skills, and conservation management supports economic diversification.

**Flagship moves:**

- Establish West Coast trades training hub in Greymouth linked to Ara Institute
- Fund NZ Conservation Corps programme creating pathways for rangatahi into conservation work
- Expand WEL Networks digital skills programme

**Tensions:**

- Small regional population limits viability of local tertiary provision
- Brain drain accelerates when local youth acquire qualifications and leave for larger centres

**Interventions on the system:**

- Establish West Coast Vocational Training Hub in Greymouth (state variable: `regional_qualification_attainment`, sign: +)
