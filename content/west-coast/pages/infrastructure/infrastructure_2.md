### State Highway Resilience and Pass Route Investment

West Coast's three alpine passes are the only road connections to the rest of NZ; NZTA investment in pass resilience and closure alternatives is critical to economic and emergency access.

**Flagship moves:**

- Upgrade Arthur's Pass and Lewis Pass resilience for extreme weather events
- Develop Haast Pass alternative routing feasibility study
- Fund permanent closure response protocols and air access contingency for multi-day closures

**Tensions:**

- Full resilience of all three passes against extreme events is prohibitively expensive
- Low traffic volumes make large capital investment hard to justify purely on transport economics

**Interventions on the system:**

- Fund West Coast alpine pass resilience programme (state variable: `pass_closure_days_per_year`, sign: -)
