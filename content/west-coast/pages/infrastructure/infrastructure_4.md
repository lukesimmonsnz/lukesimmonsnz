### Three Waters and Community Infrastructure Renewal

West Coast's small towns have ageing water and wastewater infrastructure that councils cannot afford to renew from local rates; Crown co-investment is essential.

**Flagship moves:**

- Fund three waters renewal in Westport, Hokitika, Reefton, and smaller towns
- Establish West Coast infrastructure revolving fund with Crown backstop
- Develop multi-council joint infrastructure delivery model

**Tensions:**

- Small community infrastructure is expensive per capita and has limited revenue base
- National three waters reform frameworks may not fit West Coast's unique low-density context

**Interventions on the system:**
