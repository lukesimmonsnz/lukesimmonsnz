Building a personal AI agent

Most of my focus right now is on a personalised AI agent that runs directly on my own system — not in the cloud, not through a hosted API. It’s an evolution of the earlier LAWO prototype I built to keep my websites up to date; the direction now is more general, more personal, and a lot more opinionated about staying local.

The work sits at the intersection of what I’m studying (AI, backend, systems) and what I actually want to exist: an agent I can trust to know about my work, because it physically cannot share anything outside my network.
